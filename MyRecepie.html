Poha

Poha is a traditional Indian dish made from flattened rice flakes. It's a quick and easy-to-prepare dish that involves rinsing the rice flakes, sautéing them with spices, vegetables, and often peanuts, and garnishing with fresh herbs and lemon juice. Poha has a delightful balance of textures, with soft and chewy rice flakes and a medley of savory flavors. It's a popular breakfast or snack option, loved for its simplicity and adaptability, and can be customized with various ingredients to suit personal tastes

Ingredients

  • Poha
  • Onion
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Coriander
  • Oil
  • Lemon
  • Green Chillies

Recipe

  1. Rinse and drain poha until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, green chilies, and onions.
  3. Optional: Add bell pepper and peas, sauté.
  4. Mix in turmeric, then add drained poha.
  5. Season with salt and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add lemon juice and garnish.
  7. Serve hot with optional garnishes.
